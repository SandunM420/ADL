Who We Are

Sri Lanka's Trusted Beverage Partner

For over three decades, we have been part of Sri Lanka's evolving wine and spirits industry, earning the trust of customers and business partners through dedication, consistency, and market expertise.

What began as a retail and distribution business has grown into a dynamic company with a vision for nationwide expansion. Leveraging more than 30 years of industry experience, we are now strengthening our presence through the importation and distribution of premium wines, spirits, and beverage brands across Sri Lanka.

We proudly serve a diverse customer base, including hotels, restaurants, clubs, supermarkets, liquor stores, and retail outlets throughout the island. Our extensive market knowledge, strong supply chain network, and customer-focused approach enable us to deliver exceptional products and dependable service wherever our clients operate.

As we continue to expand, our goal is to become one of Sri Lanka's leading beverage distribution partners, connecting internationally recognized brands with businesses and consumers nationwide.

Driven by experience, guided by quality, and focused on growth, we remain committed to creating lasting value for our customers, suppliers, and partners.
