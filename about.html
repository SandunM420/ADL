Who We Are

Sri Lanka's Trusted Beverage Partner

Abeywardana Distributors (Pvt) Ltd is a leading importer and distributor of premium alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages in Sri Lanka. We specialise in sourcing exceptional wines, champagnes, and spirits from the world's most prestigious producing regions and bringing them to the Sri Lankan market.

Our portfolio spans wines from Chile, Australia, South Africa, and Spain; authentic French champagnes; sparkling wines; and a comprehensive range of spirits including whiskey, rum, gin, vodka, brandy, and liqueurs. Every label in our collection is carefully selected for quality, consistency, and value.

We serve the full spectrum of the Sri Lankan beverage market — from five-star hotels and award-winning restaurants to neighbourhood retailers and individual consumers — with island-wide distribution and dedicated trade support.
